How to Play

JEPR
Strong
Strong attack. Deals 8 damage.
Light
Light attack. Deals 3 damage.
Shield
Shield / block. Heals 2 HP.
Counter
Counter. Reflects incoming strong/light x1.
Grab
Grab. Deals 6 damage.
Taunt
Taunt. Doubles next damage dealt.
Each player starts with 25 HP. Pick your moves each turn — both players act simultaneously. Reduce your opponent to 0 HP to win!

Move Matchups

Light beats Strong (faster speed)
Light, Strong, or Grab beat Taunt
